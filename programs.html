The Movewell Programs

Other clinics sell you an hour, we help you achieve the result.

Three distinct programs, matched to where you are and how far you want to go.

Every program is built around one idea: your recovery shouldn't pause when you walk out the door. Between every visit you have a physical therapist in your pocket, a program that updates week by week as you adapt, and a direct line to your provider when something comes up. One-on-one sessions are where we perform ongoing assessments, manage any flare-ups, introduce new movements and challenges, and adjust your programming based on how you're responding. Following consistent, progressive programming between those visits is where your change really happens. The only difference between programs is how far you need to go.

Restore

6-Week Program

For the mostly healthy athlete dealing with a minor sprain or strain, or someone finishing the last stretch of a rehab they started elsewhere. Six weeks of focused work to close the gap and get back to training at full capacity.

What runs the whole time

  • ✓Weekly progressive programming in the CoachRx app, updated as you adapt
  • ✓Direct messaging access to your provider between every visit
  • ✓Video demonstrations and coaching cues for each exercise
  • ✓Baseline force-plate and strength testing at your evaluation
  • ✓Objective re-test at program completion so you can see exactly how far you came

Includes 5 one-on-one sessions with your physical therapist, scheduled up front and tapering toward the end as your programming takes over.

$1,395

Build

12-Week Program

For anyone dealing with a recurring issue or coming back from something that took real capacity away. Twelve weeks to rebuild the strength and tissue tolerance to not just feel better, but genuinely trust your body under load again.

What runs the whole time

  • ✓Weekly progressive programming in the CoachRx app, updated as you adapt
  • ✓Direct messaging access to your provider between every visit
  • ✓Video demonstrations and coaching cues for each exercise
  • ✓Force-plate and strength testing at baseline, week 6, and week 12
  • ✓Criteria-based progression so you advance based on what the testing shows, not guesswork

Includes 7 one-on-one sessions with your physical therapist, scheduled up front and tapering toward the end as your programming takes over.

$2,295

Peak

16-Week Program

For the full return to sport, late-stage post-operative rehab, or anyone who wants to develop performance well beyond where they started. Sixteen weeks that take you from recovered to genuinely ready for whatever comes next.

What runs the whole time

  • ✓Weekly progressive programming in the CoachRx app, updated as you adapt
  • ✓Direct messaging access to your provider between every visit
  • ✓Video demonstrations and coaching cues for each exercise
  • ✓Force-plate and strength testing every four weeks throughout the program
  • ✓Return-to-performance criteria so clearance is based on what the numbers show, not the calendar
  • ✓Transition into ongoing Movewell Performance Programming when you finish

Includes 9 one-on-one sessions with your physical therapist, scheduled up front and tapering toward the end as your programming takes over.

$2,995

You get a result, one-on-one — plus programming and progress that don't stop when you leave the facility.

Every program begins with a physical therapy evaluation $249 including baseline objective testing with dynamometry and force plates where applicable, for measurable, trackable results. That evaluation is where we assess your presentation and recommend the right program for where you are. Programming runs between every visit through the CoachRx app. Payment plans are available on Build and Peak. When your formal episode of care wraps up, you can carry the momentum forward with Movewell Performance Programming — the same coaching and the same app, built around what comes next for you.
