Services

Every entry point leads to the same place.

Whether you're injured, recovering, or training — there's a place for you at MoveWell. Every service is built around the same idea: rehab and performance aren't separate. They're a continuum.

1-on-1 Physical Therapy

Most Comprehensive

IN-PERSON · HYBRID MODEL

This isn't the PT you've done before. Every plan combines hands-on in-person sessions with Performance Continuity Programming (PCP) — structured, progressive work delivered between visits so progress doesn't stop when the session ends. We don't just get you out of pain. We build you past it.

Ideal for: Those dealing with active pain, recovering from injury, or looking to finally bridge the gap between rehab and real performance.

Performance Training

IN-PERSON · 1-ON-1 OR SMALL GROUP

High-level, individualized coaching focused on strength, conditioning, and movement quality. Every session is built around you — your history, your goals, your capacity. No guessing, no generic programs. Just coaching that actually moves the needle.

Ideal for: Healthy or pain-free individuals who want hands-on coaching, real accountability, and a training system built around performance.

Online Rehab Coaching

REMOTE · FULLY VIRTUAL

Pain and injury don't wait for you to have access to a clinic. Our online rehab coaching brings the same structured, progressive approach to wherever you are — including a movement assessment, a clear rehab plan, and ongoing communication so you're never left guessing what to do next.

Ideal for: Those dealing with pain or injury who can't attend in person and want expert guidance without the guesswork.

Online Performance Programming

REMOTE · APP-BASED

A high-level training system with the flexibility to fit your life. Delivered through our app with clear programming, built-in progressions, exercise demos, and performance tracking. No randomness, no winging it — just structured work that compounds over time.

Ideal for: Self-motivated individuals who want a serious training system and the independence to execute on their own terms.
