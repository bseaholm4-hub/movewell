Services

Every entry point leads to the same place.

Whether you're injured, recovering, or training, there's a place for you at Movewell. Every service is built around the same idea: rehab and performance aren't separate. They're a continuum.

1-on-1 Physical Therapy

Most Comprehensive

IN-PERSON · HYBRID MODEL

Most PT stops at pain relief but ours doesn't. Your in-person sessions are paired with structured programming between visits, we call it Performance Continuity Programming, so your progress keeps moving whether you're with us or not. The goal isn't just getting you out of pain, it's getting you back to the things you actually care about + moving better than you did before you got hurt.

Ideal for: Those dealing with active pain, recovering from injury, or looking to finally bridge the gap between rehab and real performance.

Performance Training

IN-PERSON · HYBRID MODEL

We take into consideration your injury history, your training background, what your goals are, and where your body is right now. From there we build strength, conditioning, and movement quality around you, not a template or a generic plan. Your programming should actually change how you feel and move every day, not just in the gym.

Ideal for: Healthy or pain-free individuals who want hands-on coaching, real accountability, and a training system built around performance.

Online Rehab Coaching

REMOTE · APP-BASED

Pain and injury don't wait, and your recovery shouldn't either. Our online coaching gives you a real plan no matter where you are. We start with a movement assessment, build a program specific to what you're dealing with, and check in consistently so nothing stalls out. This is how recovery coaching should work, it just hasn't been accessible like this until now.

Ideal for: Those dealing with pain or injury who can't attend in person and want expert guidance without the guesswork. Note that this is not formal medical care.

Online Performance Programming

REMOTE · APP-BASED

A training system built for people who want real structure without needing to be in the same room. Your programming is delivered through our app with clear progressions, exercise demos, performance tracking, and more. You can walk into the gym knowing exactly what you're doing, you track everything, and your coach applies progressive overload week to week so the work actually compounds over time!

Ideal for: Self-motivated individuals who want a serious training system and the independence to execute on their own terms.

Conditions We Treat

We treat the injuries and limitations that active people can't afford to ignore. We work best with people who are dealing with pain that's gotten in the way of training, sport, or just living actively — and who want a plan that actually gets them back to full capacity, not just comfortable.

Musculoskeletal Pain & Injury

Muscle strains, ligament sprains, shoulder pain, elbow pain, hip pain, low back pain, knee pain, neck pain, foot & ankle pain.

Tendinopathies

Patellar, achilles, proximal hamstring, rotator cuff. Tendinopathies are load management problems, and that's exactly how we treat them. No passive modalities, no guesswork — just progressive loading protocols built around where your tendon actually is right now.

Running & Overuse Injuries

Shin splints, stress reactions, IT band syndrome, plantar fasciitis, and more. Most running injuries come down to load, capacity, and timing — not your form. We find the actual driver and build you back up with a plan that keeps you moving as much as possible in the process.

Hip Impingement & FAI

Common in lifters, cyclists, and athletes — and frequently mismanaged. We assess what's actually limiting you, address the strength and mobility deficits contributing to it, and get you back to training without working around it forever.

Nerve-Related Pain

Sciatica, thoracic outlet syndrome, piriformis syndrome, and other nerve-driven presentations. These get misdiagnosed and mistreated constantly. We find the source, not just the symptom.

Prehab & Pre-Surgical Optimization

The stronger and more prepared you go into a procedure, the better you come out. If surgery is on the table, working with us beforehand is one of the highest-leverage things you can do for your outcome.

ACL Rehab

One of the most undertreated injuries in terms of true return-to-sport readiness. We take ACL rehab through every phase — from early post-op through full return to cutting, loading, and competing — with criteria-based progressions, not just timelines.

Post-Surgical Rehab

Surgery gets you the structure. We build back the strength, movement quality, and capacity around it. Whether you're weeks out or months out and feeling stuck, we'll meet you where you are.

Return to Sport & Performance

For athletes and active adults who've cleared the acute phase but aren't back to full training. We bridge the gap between "feeling okay" and actually performing — with progressive loading, sport-specific demands, and objective benchmarks that tell you when you're ready.
