Online Performance Programming

Online Performance Programming is designed for individuals who want expert-built, structured training with the flexibility to train anywhere, anytime. This is not a generic template. Your programming is individualized, progressive, and built around your goals, training history, and available equipment.

What to Expect

  • Personalized Training Plan: We start with an intake form to learn more about your background, then schedule a call to discuss your goals, experience level, schedule, and equipment access. Once a start date is set, your program is built intentionally to ensure measurable progression.
  • Delivered Through the CoachRx App: Access all workouts, exercise demonstrations, notes, and progress tracking in one place. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.
  • Ongoing Support & Adjustments: Stay connected through regular check-ins, feedback loops, and programming updates based on how you’re responding to training. Adjustments are made as your goals evolve or life demands shift.

Is Online Performance Programming a Good Fit?

  • You want structured, guided programming without needing consistent in-person sessions.
  • You have clear goals: build strength, improve performance, stay consistent, or train around minor aches and want a plan that evolves with you.
  • You value accountability, progress tracking, and flexibility in where and when you train.
