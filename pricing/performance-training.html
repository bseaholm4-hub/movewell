Performance Training

Our 1-on-1 performance training is designed for active individuals who want to build strength, move well, and continue progressing long term. Whether you're competing in-season, lifting recreationally, or prioritizing your health, we meet you where you are and provide structured guidance to help you improve.

What to Expect

  • Individualized Strength & Conditioning: Your program is built around your goals, training history, and movement profile. We focus on improving movement quality, strength, and overall capacity - not just completing workouts.
  • 1-on-1 Coaching: Every session is coached with attention to detail. We teach, cue, adjust, and progress you appropriately, including modifying around previous injuries or current limitations when needed.
  • Structured, Progressive Training: Training follows organized cycles designed to build strength, work capacity, and durability over time. The goal is measurable, sustainable progress that carries over to sport and daily life, not random intensity.

We Could Be a Good Fit If…

  • You want professional guidance rather than programming yourself.
  • You’re returning from an injury, or managing ongoing aches and pains, and want to train more intelligently without constant setbacks.
  • You care about building strength, athleticism, and durability so your body can handle both training and daily life demands.
