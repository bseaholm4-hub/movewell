Online Rehab Coaching

Online Rehab Coaching provides expert guidance and structured programming for individuals dealing with pain or injury who don't need—or don't have access to—frequent in-person physical therapy. We bridge the gap between traditional rehab and full performance training.

What to Expect

  • Comprehensive Assessment & Planning: We start with an in-depth video consultation (45-60 min) to review your history, assess movement, and clarify goals. From there, your rehab program is built specifically for your needs.
  • Delivered Through the CoachRx App: All programming is delivered through an easy-to-use app where you can log sessions, track progress, and communicate directly with your coach.

Is Online Rehab Coaching a Good Fit?

  • You’re dealing with pain, discomfort, or a past injury limiting daily life or gym training.
  • You’ve completed physical therapy (or don’t require in-person care) but aren’t ready for full performance training.
  • You want structured, progressive guidance with a rehab focus.
  • You value individualized support from a Doctor of Physical Therapy in a non-clinical coaching format.
