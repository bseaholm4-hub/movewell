Online Rehab Coaching

Online Rehab Coaching is built around the part of recovery that usually gets left out. Most rehab happens in a clinic for an hour and then leaves you on your own until the next visit, when the days in between are really where your body adapts and gets stronger. We put that part first. You get a plan that keeps progressing as you do, a Doctor of Physical Therapy building and adjusting it around your actual life, and a direct line to us when something flares up or you are not sure about a movement.

For a lot of people dealing with pain or a lingering injury, that steady guidance is exactly what has been missing. Getting out of pain matters, but on its own it is closer to a quarter of the job than the whole thing, and staying better comes from the capacity you rebuild in the weeks that follow. This is how we help you do that without needing to be in the room, wherever you happen to be.

What to Expect

  • Comprehensive Assessment & Planning: We start with an in-depth video consultation (45-60 min) to review your history, assess movement, and clarify goals. From there, your rehab program is built specifically for your needs.
  • Delivered Through the CoachRx App: All programming is delivered through an easy-to-use app where you can log sessions, track progress, and communicate directly with your coach.

Is Online Rehab Coaching a Good Fit?

  • You’re dealing with pain, discomfort, or a past injury limiting daily life or gym training.
  • You’ve completed physical therapy (or don’t require in-person care) but aren’t ready for full performance training.
  • You want structured, progressive guidance with a rehab focus.
  • You value individualized support from a Doctor of Physical Therapy in a non-clinical coaching format.
