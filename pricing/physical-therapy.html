Physical Therapy

Whether you’re recovering from an injury, managing persistent pain, or feeling limited in your movement, our physical therapy process is designed to help you return to what you love. We take a hands‑on, movement‑based approach to address the root causes of your challenges and guide you with a personalized plan that blends rehab to performance.

What to Expect

  • Initial Evaluation (90 minutes): A comprehensive, one‑on‑one assessment diving into your subjective history, current routine, recovery habits, movement, strength, mobility, and more to identify key issues and create a personalized care plan.
  • Follow‑Up Sessions (60 minutes): A blend of hands‑on manual therapy, exercise, movement retraining, and load‑based strategies to help you move and feel your best.
  • Education & Long‑Term Strategy: Leave each session understanding your body better; with a plan to build strength, lessen the chance for re‑injury, and return to the activities you care about.

We Could Be a Good Fit If…

  • You’re dealing with pain or injury and want a comprehensive approach that blends hands-on treatment with progressive, movement-based rehab.
  • You’re frustrated by recurring symptoms and want to address the underlying causes, not just temporarily reduce discomfort.
  • You want dedicated, one-on-one time to fully assess what’s going on and build a structured plan tailored to your goals.
  • You plan to return to lifting, running, sport, or an active lifestyle and want guidance that bridges rehab back to real performance with confidence.
