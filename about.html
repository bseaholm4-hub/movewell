Our Philosophy

Most people accept pain and stiffness as inevitable. They modify their lives around what their body won't do. Goals shrink. They stop playing, stop moving, stop trusting themselves.

We don't accept that.

Movewell builds bodies that last — through individualized care and progressive training that matches the real demands of your life. We look at the whole picture: how you're loading, how you're moving, what your body is prepared to tolerate.

We're not chasing symptoms. We're building capacity.

The result isn't just durability. It's capability. And capability unlocks the freedom to move without hesitation.

Meet the Team

Jimmy

Doctor of Physical Therapy, Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist

Jimmy is a Doctor of Physical Therapy, Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist, and founder of Movewell. He works with athletes and active adults who want to get out of pain and return to training stronger and more confident.

His approach blends physical therapy and performance training, focusing on building strength, capacity, and long-term durability — not just short-term fixes.

Jimmy earned his DPT from Rosalind Franklin University and began his career at Illinois Bone & Joint Institute before starting MoveWell in Chicago.
